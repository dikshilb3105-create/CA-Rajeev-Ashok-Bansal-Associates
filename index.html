About Our Firm

Delivering reliable, ethical, and timely financial solutions for over 20 years.

Rajeev Ashok Bansal & Associates is a professional Chartered Accountancy firm specializing in Audit, Taxation, Compliance, and Advisory Services for businesses, startups, and individuals. We are committed to delivering reliable, ethical, and timely financial solutions tailored to diverse client needs.

With a strong focus on professionalism, integrity, and quality service, we aim to build long-term relationships based on trust and value creation.

The firm is led by CA. Rajeev Bansal, who has been practicing since 2003, bringing over 20+ years of extensive experience in accounting, auditing, taxation, and financial advisory.

An alumnus of Shri Guru Teg Bahadur Khalsa College, University of Delhi, he represents a strong foundation of academic excellence and professional expertise.

Beyond his professional practice, he actively contributes as an Executive Member of Indirect Taxes Consultants Association, Ludhiana, reflecting his commitment to continuous learning, collaboration, and advancement of the profession.
