About Our Firm

Delivering reliable, ethical, and timely financial solutions for over 20 years.

Rajeev Ashok Bansal & Associates is a professional Chartered Accountancy firm specializing in audit, taxation, compliance, and advisory services for businesses, startups, and individuals. The firm is committed to delivering reliable, ethical, and timely financial solutions tailored to the evolving needs of clients across diverse industries.

With a strong focus on professionalism, integrity, and quality service, we strive to build long-term relationships based on trust and value creation.

The firm is led by its Founder and Proprietor, who has been practicing as a Chartered Accountant since 2003, bringing over two decades of extensive professional experience in accounting, auditing, taxation, and financial advisory services.

He is a graduate of Shri Guru Teg Bahadur Khalsa College, Delhi University, one of the premier institutions known for academic excellence and professional development.

In addition to his professional practice, he actively contributes to the professional community as an Executive Member of Team ITCA Ludhiana, demonstrating his commitment to continuous learning, professional collaboration, and the advancement of the Chartered Accountancy profession.
